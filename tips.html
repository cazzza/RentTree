Tips for Reducing Carbon Emissions

  • Switch to LED light bulbs
  • Use energy-efficient appliances (look for Energy Star ratings)
  • Adjust thermostat settings to reduce heating and cooling needs
  • Unplug electronics and appliances when not in use
  • Use natural light when possible
  • Improve home insulation
  • Use cold water for laundry when possible
  • Regular maintenance of HVAC systems

Remember, small changes can add up to significant reductions in your carbon footprint over time!
