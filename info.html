Understanding Carbon Emissions

Carbon emissions refer to the release of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. These emissions contribute to global warming and climate change.

Common Sources of Household Emissions:

  • Heating and cooling systems
  • Lighting
  • Large appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, etc.)
  • Electronics
  • Water heating

By understanding and tracking these emissions, we can take steps to reduce our carbon footprint and contribute to a more sustainable future.
